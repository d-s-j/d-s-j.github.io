I'm a fullstack software engineer from Richmond, BC, Canada & co-creator of Synapse with a background in Graphic Design and Illustrations.

Synapse is an open-source tool to build, deploy, and monitor a GraphQL Gateway, putting legacy APIs behind a single GraphQL endpoint.

Synapse is able to do this without touching or altering your underlying data sources, and is built with React, Express, MongoDB, and few more additional tools.
